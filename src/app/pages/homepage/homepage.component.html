WELCOME JJ DIAGNOSTIC CENTER

JJ Diagnostic center India Speciality Diagnostic centre is one of the eminent, reliable and fastest growing Healthcare Service Provider in Central Travancore. Headquartered in Mavelikara, the centre now includes nearly 100 employees with specialists such as Radiologist, Pathologist and Cardiologist.

ESTABLISHMENT & GROWTH

In the year 2000 JJ Diagnsostic Center India Speciality Diagnostic Centre was established as a small-scale firm and has since become a fully established centre in the year 2003 In the year 2012, two branches have been opened at Olakettiyambalam and Kurathikaadu, third branch was started at Parumalakadavu, Mannar in 2015 while a fourth branch was started at Punnamoodu in 2021.

QUALITY OBJECTIVE

To comply with ISO 15189:2012 standards and NABL requirements at all times Continuously assess and improve the performance of the laboratory Constantly motivate and train staff for effective implementation of quality systems Will ensure compliance with statutory, local and national regulations at all times To ensure satisfaction of all our clients
